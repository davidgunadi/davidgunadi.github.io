Introduction

Hi there, thank you for taking the time to apply. You can find our application process below.

We understand that everyone's time is important, thus we aim to complete the whole process within 5 business days starting from the day you completed the assessment, as indicated in the timeline below.

Once again we thank you for your time.

STEP 1 - Initial Assessment (Day 1)

As a screening step for the Head of Engineering position with McEasy, we require each candidate to go through an Initial Assessment (unique link will be provided to the candidate).

You will start by:

    - a quick 60 seconds self introduction video recording
    - followed by a series of 5 x 10 minutes assessments for various topics (technical and non-technical)

STEP 2 - Interview with our VP of Engineering (Day 2-3)

Once you completed the assessment, you will be invited to a one hour interview (including a 15 minutes live coding interview using a language of your choice).

STEP 3 - Interview with our Founders (Day 4-5)

The last step would be a direct interview with our Founder, to ensure that McEasy is a good fit for you.

STEP 4 - Welcome to the Team

Looking forward to working with you!
